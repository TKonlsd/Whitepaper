Adventure Mode

Adventure Mode will be the single-player feature of this game. By playing it, the player will be able to go through story stages and clear them to find out more about Herobots: Omens of Hero’s lore. Upon beating Adventure Mode, the Endless Arena will be unlocked, where the player will be able to fight against the AI to their heart’s content.
