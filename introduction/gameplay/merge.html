Merge

Players will be able to merge spare Herobots into new and different ones, which will inherit random pieces and stats from the originals. To use this feature, two Herobots will be chosen to use their parts and gens to create a brand new one, which parts, stats, and gens will depend on those its predecessors held.
There will be different levels of merging, allowing players to spend more expensive and rare materials to invest in a higher-tier merge, however, they will also need to meet the level requirements.
