Mechanics

Herobots: Omens of Hero features a new NFT game model, aiming to offer a free-to-play experience, focusing on being a fun, deep, and entertaining videogame while adding the feature of play-and-earn, meaning this will be an extra that adds to the game, but not the main purpose of it. However, players will still have the chance to trade at the marketplace and earn rewards as they play (further check the Economics section to find out more about this).
When fighting, you’ll need to set up your team of 3 Herobots and choose the cards you get from your Herobot pieces before engaging in the battle, allowing you to choose your strategy of choice and prepare your deck before every match.
