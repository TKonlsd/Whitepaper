Earn

​
As an NFT and Play-To-Earn game, Herobots: Omens of Hero will reward its players, but it will do so in a sustainable way and avoid the pitfalls of other systems in the past.
Players will earn in three different ways:
  • Selling game items in the marketplace.
  • Daily missions where we share part of the proceeds coming from the token transaction fees.
  • HROS tokens for best players in top leagues of P2P modes.
These rewards will be granted by sharing a certain % of the volume with all players that clear said missions. There will also be rewards for players who win matches in high rank leagues, as there will be also the chance to trade and make some profit at the marketplace.
