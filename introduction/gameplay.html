Gameplay

Herobots: Omens Of Hero offers multiple and different game modes and features, so make sure to explore them all to enjoy the game at its fullest
HEROBOTS: Omens Of Hero gameplay footage - DEMO version
Upon pulling new Herobots cores, players will obtain a full new Herobot consisting of random pieces among all existing ones from every Herobot type, resulting in a very exclusive and unique one.
Each of these pieces has its own stats, which along with the Herobot class, will determine the final result. Aside from this, each of the pieces will also come with two cards, which you can use to prepare your Herobot deck to use to fight. Before every battle, you’ll need to choose the cards you will be using so you get to draw them each turn and play your hand the best you can think of. Each card will have an energy cost, meaning you’ll need to think wisely about which ones to play each turn.
