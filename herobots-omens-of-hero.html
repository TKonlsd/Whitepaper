BLIGHT:SURVIVAL: Test of Time

BLIGHT:SURVIVAL: Test of Time is a turn-based battle NFT game with a sustainable 'Play to Earn' model, featuring different game modes such as Adventure, Battle, or Slums Brawl.
To join the game, a limited run of Operators will be offered for players to purchase and dispose of at launch. Operators purchased in this way will be randomly generated by unique combinations of parts, core, and genes, thus forming an NFT.
This online game will allow the player to earn tokens while completing certain missions. In order to compete it is necessary to buy Operators to form a complete squad composed of 3 Operators with which you can play in any of the game modes offered or trade with them in the Marketplace In addition to this, the game will feature an elaborate crafting and merge system, allowing players to also trade other items and materials that will be valuable to these mechanics.
Each of these pieces has its own stats, which along with the core type, will determine the final properties of the resulting Operator
