Alpha v0.0.3

​
- Fixed some visual errors regarding some Herobot parts.
- Fixed some visual errors regarding attack animations.
- Fixed resolution for tablet devices.
- Added auxiliary status windows feature to check the target’s stats, active buffs and debuffs with detailed information about their effects and duration by tapping/clicking on target's health bar.
- Added shadow effects.
- Added tutorial to main menu.
- Chinese version release.
- Improved server data reading system.
- Improved Herobots and enemies sizes and positions.
- Changes in card text boxes to make differences between rounds and Herobots turns.
- Changed card draw system to always draw up to 3 cards when you lose allies.
- Cards balance:
  • Barrage: Damage increased from 250 to 350.
  • Damage transfer: Now deals double the damage taken by the last attack received. Energy cost increased from 10 to 20.
  • Devastating shot: Damage increased from 500 to 700.
  • Laser recoil: Damage increased from 300 to 500.
  • Mark prey: Damage increased from 300 to 400.
  • Piercing shots: Damage increased from 300 to 500.
