Gameplay

BLIGHT:SURVIVAL: Test of Time offers multiple and different game modes and features, so make sure to explore them all to enjoy the game at its fullest
BLIGHT:SURVIVAL: Test of Time gameplay footage - DEMO version
Upon pulling new Operators cores, players will obtain a full new Operator consisting of random pieces among all existing ones from every Operator type, resulting in a very exclusive and unique one.
Each of these pieces has its own stats, which along with the Operator class, will determine the final result. Aside from this, each of the pieces will also come with two cards, which you can use to prepare your Operator deck to use to fight. Before every battle, you’ll need to choose the cards you will be using so you get to draw them each turn and play your hand the best you can think of. Each card will have an energy cost, meaning you’ll need to think wisely about which ones to play each turn.
